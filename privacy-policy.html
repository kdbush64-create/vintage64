At Vintage64TX, your privacy is a core component of our security-first philosophy. This policy outlines how we handle data for individuals, solopreneurs, and small partnerships seeking IT services in Fort Worth, TX and the broader DFW Metroplex.

1. DATA COLLECTION PROTOCOLS

We only collect data that is strictly necessary for providing IT services. This includes:

  • Identity Data: Name and business entity type (LLC, Partnership, etc.).
  • Contact Data: Email address and phone number provided via Cal.com.
  • Technical Data: IP addresses and browser types collected by Cloudflare for security and performance.
  • Service Logs: Hardware specs or diagnostic logs shared during a Phoenix Protocol Linux migration or system hardening engagement.

2. THIRD-PARTY UPLINKS

We do not host your sensitive payment or scheduling data on our own servers. We utilize industry-standard partners:

  • Cal.com: Manages our booking and scheduling.
  • Stripe: Processes all payments. We never see or store your credit card numbers.
  • Malwarebytes / ESET: Used for license fulfillment at your request.

3. TEXAS DATA PRIVACY COMPLIANCE (TDPSA)

In accordance with the Texas Data Privacy and Security Act (TDPSA), we provide the following disclosures:

  • No Sale of Data: Vintage64TX DOES NOT SELL your personal or sensitive data to third parties.
  • Right to Access/Delete: You may request a copy of your data or its deletion at any time by contacting us directly.
  • Targeted Advertising: We do not use your data for targeted third-party advertising or automated profiling.

4. DATA SECURITY

As a CompTIA Security+ certified IT provider based in Fort Worth, TX, we implement "Gold Standard" protections including encrypted data transfers (HTTPS), multi-factor authentication (MFA) on all business tools, and strict data minimization — if we don't need it to fix your machine, we don't ask for it.

5. CONTACT & INQUIRIES

For questions regarding these protocols or to exercise your privacy rights under the TDPSA, please initiate contact via the main contact page or email dewayne@vintage64tx.com.
